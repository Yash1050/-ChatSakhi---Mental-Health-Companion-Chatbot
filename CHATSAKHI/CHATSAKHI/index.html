WELCOME TO CHATSAKHI: YOUR PARTNER IN POSTPARTUM DEPRESSION

Welcome to CHATSAKHI, where we stand beside every new and first-time mother navigating the challenging path of postpartum depression. We offer a one-stop solution, providing information, professional connections, and practical lifestyle guidance to make your journey towards recovery more manageable. Our website features a friendly chatbot available around the clock, ready to listen and provide timely reassurances, practical advice, and a soothing presence when immediate help isn't available. Our testing portal empowers you to assess the presence and severity of postpartum depression, guiding you towards the next steps in your healing journey. Beyond clinical support, we believe that healing extends to simple lifestyle changes that can significantly impact your overall well-being. We're honored to be your partner on the path to improved mental health.
